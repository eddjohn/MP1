About Us

For over 7 years, Super EJ’s Bicycle Store is recognized as the most affordable quality bicycle set and parts. We’ve built our reputation on treating our customers like family by offering premium products and trustworthy after-sales service from our suppliers that are dedicated to our customer and their needs. We make great efforts to introduce a new line of products and services to customers and continue to provide innovation in ensuring the efficiency of products.

We aim to provide good quality items at a very affordable price

We want to create a society by using cycling to improve health and economic stability. We also promote cycling as social, sustainable and green mode of transportation. Whatever your destination, we help you to have the best possible cycling experience for boundless enjoyment.
